Description

The pie is a traditional dish , often sweet in nature ,and is enjoyed be people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • apple
  • maple syrup
  • Flour
  • Baking soda

Steps to make the dish

  1. Make the flour fluffy
  2. Cut some apple slices and use them in the flour
  3. Add some baking soda to the mixture
  4. Put it in oven for 5 min at 350
  5. Add maple syrup at the end
